\b;Objective

Move the robot according to the values obtained.

\t;Instructions

From this mission you will begin to use the structure \c;\l;if/else\u cbot\if;\n;

Capture the value of \type;v1\norm; (type \type;int\norm;) from the exchange post.

1) If the value of \type;v1\norm; is greater than or equal to 5, move 5 meters north; if it is less than 5, move 5 meters toward the east;

The following functions will be used in this mission:
   \c;\l;move\u cbot\move;\n; - moves forward or backward;
   \c;\l;turn\u cbot\turn;\n; - rotates according to the given angle;
   \c;\l;receive\u cbot\receive;\n; - requests information from a post.
