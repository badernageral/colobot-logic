\b;Objective

Collect all the blue crosses.

\t;Instructions

The category of blue crosses is \type;WayPoint\norm;.

Use the \c;\l;radar\u cbot\radar;\n; function to locate the nearest cross and move to it with \c;\l;goto\u cbot\goto;\n;.

Remember that the cross is an object and its position can be accessed through the \const;position\norm; property.

The following functions will be used in this mission:
   \c;\l;goto\u cbot\goto;\n; - moves to the informed position;
   \c;\l;radar\u cbot\radar;\n; - captures information from a nearby object.
